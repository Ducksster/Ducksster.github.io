Moving into dorms with NKU ROCKS

On August 15th I moved into NKU. I moved in earlier than most others because I moved in with NKUROCKS, a student lead organization. NKUROCKS works to help Black students get adjusted with college life by teaching and supporting each other. NKUROCKS is a UMI (You Matter Institute) organization. UMI leads multiple organizations that help with diversity equity and inclusion including ROCKS, Q+, and LAMP. When I came to campus NKU ROCKS and UMI and an entire week or activities and socializing planned. While there I met multiple people that became friends such as Everett, Achilles and, Areli. There are also a bunch of friends I met from UMI after the first week. I definitely reccommend UMI organiztions for any sudents that are interested in something like that. Unfortunately, organiztionslike that are under attack but I know many of the people that run these groups and they won't go down without a fight.
